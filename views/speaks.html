Rwanda Speaks in partnership with Imbuto foundation.

Aspire Debate Rwanda in partnership with Imbuto foundation joined their efforts to empower Rwandan Youth and promote debate culture among. The Rwanda Speaks program is taking place in a total of 17 youth centers and AERG hostel. Aspire Debate Rwanda and Imbuto foundation and are reaching out to marginalized youth in order to cultivate their critical thinking, public speaking skills and build their confidence.

The trainers visit the centers on a weekly basis, conducting debate workshops tackling different issues and raising awareness about various topics concerning the youth and their personal and social development. The methodology also seeks to engage the youth creatively in developing their own solutions within their communities and local authorities, and giving them a voice in shaping their vision and involvement in the future of the world they live in.
