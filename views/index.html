ABOUT US

Aspire Debate Rwanda is community based organization that was founded in 2014, by group of university youth volunteers, with zeal to educate, empower, enhance and promote public speaking, communication skills, leadership skills and critical thinking capacities among youth. ADR looks forward to be the foremost provider of debate education and training to young people across the region in the near future.

We partner with, support and inspire individuals, schools, universities or institutions devoted to giving students access to debate and speech development. Aspire Debate Rwanda also believes that the art of debate promotes mutual understanding and informed citizenship around the world and also leads to increased critical thinking, analysis and tolerance, enhanced cultural exchange and greater academic excellence

OUR VALUES

Aspire to be Informed, Inspired and Involved

Why debate?

Debate develops critical thinking skills, improve your active listening skills, instills confidence, boosts your language proficiency and knowledge that will help the debater become an elite and college-ready student.

Vision

To educate, empower, enhance and promote public speaking, communication skills, leadership skills and critical thinking capacities among youth.

Mission

The mission of Aspire Debate Rwanda is to prepare youth to be intellectually vibrant, be self-resilient and self-reliant
