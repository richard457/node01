East African University Debate Championship

After attending the 2016 Pan African University Debate Champion in ACCRA-Ghana, we came up with an idea of organising East Africa University Debate Champion (EAUDC) as an annual event together with the East African youth ambassadors platform Rwanda chapter which was launched

during the 2nd EAC University Studentsâ€™ Debate in September 2013 to facilitate structured engagement with the Youth. The Youth in East African Community (EAC) constitute more than 63% of the population. Article 120 (c) of the EAC Treaty provides for adoption of common approach for involvement of the youth in the integration process and this article does not only aim at strengthening the bond and synergy of East African youth but also energizes the spirit of Pan Africanism.

GENERAL OBJECTIVE

The major goal of the East Africa University Debate Championship is to enhance and enrich a continuous cooperation amongst university students and youths from the partner states and also entice them to heavily advocate for regional integration programs/agenda.

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES:

  • To gather a catalogue of ideas from youth for East African Community
  • To create awareness on East African Community
  • To increase the bond and create a networking platform among regional youths.
  • To enhance leadership potential among the African youth.
