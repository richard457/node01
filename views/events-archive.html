WORK WITH US

Aspire Debate Rwanda is pleasured and honored to work with a diversity of group of people from all levels of professionalism. To lend a hand in our activities or program, can be done in any of the following ways delineated below.

  • As a Mentor
  • As an adjudicator
  • As a volunteer
  • As a donor
WHERE CAN YOU FIND US

The headquarter of ADR are located in UR-CST (former KIST) next to English language department and you can also find our services at any of university debate clubs that we work with i.e. UR-CE, UR-CBE, UR-CASS, UR-Nyagatare Campus, UR-CAVM, UR-CST, UNILAK-Kigali, ULK-Kigali, ULK-Gisenyi, KEPLER University, University of Gitwe, ICK-Kabyayi, IPRC-Kigali, RTUC-Kigali, MUK,and KIM University.

WHY PARTNER WITH US

Working in partnership with us can fetch long-lasting benefits to your company/institution/ organization whilst helping to raise vital funds to support promotion of debating culture in Rwanda. This is why you should partner with us.

  • Meet Corporate Social Responsibility objectives
  • Help debate culture grow and spread in the communities in which you operate
  • Reach new audiences and drive sales
  • Secure positive Public Relations
  • Build brand profile
  • Stand out in a competitive market
